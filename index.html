Collar Features

General Features

  • A single, robust GPS and VHF housing – no wires running through the collar.
  • Highly accurate GPS and reliable Iridium & Globalstar transmission, even when housing is under the neck.
  • Completely independent VHF system with its own batteries and PCB – unmatched reliability.
  • Dual GPS and Iridium antennas for stronger, faster communication.
  • Heavy-duty belting that is 4× stronger than competitors.
  • Ergonomic bolt placement at the top of the collar for better fit and no breakage.
  • One-size-fits-all design – only need the average neck size.

Iridium Collars (Powered by Xeos Technologies)

  • High-voltage system – superior performance and cold-weather reliability.
  • True 2-way communication – adjust GPS/Iridium schedules instantly without manufacturer contact.
  • Optional VHF integration.
  • Higher temporal resolution for precise tracking.
  • 5-year lifespan.

Globalstar Collars (Powered by GeoForce)

  • Dual redundant devices – always one device facing skyward, even if collar rotates.
  • Optional single-device configuration.
  • Optional VHF integration.
  • Reports location every 12 hours per device (4 daily with staggered scheduling).
  • Lightweight, simple design with a 5-year lifespan.
